📋 Legal & Work Permit Roadmap

1

6-Month Waiting Period

D-4 (Language Training) visa holders must complete 6 months of study before applying for part-time work permission.

2

School Permission Required

You must obtain written permission from your school's international office before applying to immigration.

3

GPA Requirement

Maintain a minimum GPA of 2.0 (C average) to remain eligible for part-time work.

4
